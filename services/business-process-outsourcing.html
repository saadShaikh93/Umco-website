Overview

Business Process Outsourcing

Hiring, training, and retaining a finance team is expensive — and a single departure can stall your month-end close. UMCO's outsourcing practice gives you a complete finance function staffed by qualified professionals, at a fraction of the in-house cost.

Every client receives a dedicated engagement manager, documented processes, and reporting calibrated to what your leadership team needs to see.

What's included

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Full-cycle transaction processing, reconciliations, and month-end close on cloud platforms.

Payroll Management

End-to-end payroll with statutory deductions, EOBI/social security, and confidential disbursement.

Management Reporting

Monthly packs with P&L, cash flow, and KPI commentary your board can act on.

CFO-as-a-Service
