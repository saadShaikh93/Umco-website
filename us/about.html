Our story

The firm is new. The judgment behind it is not.

Before this firm existed, its founder spent fifteen years in professional practice where finance crosses borders — external audits of subsidiaries of international groups, regulatory and compliance work in the Gulf, outsourced delivery for professional firms as far away as Canada. Different countries, one operating principle: the senior professional who wins the work should be the one who does it.

That vantage point kept exposing the same gap. Clients on every side of those engagements eventually needed the American half handled — a US entity formed, a Form 5472 filed, a treaty read correctly — and kept finding that firms fluent on both sides of a border barely exist.

Umair Manzoor PC was licensed by the Montana Board of Public Accountants in 2026 to work exactly that seam: deliberately new, deliberately focused, and founder-led by design — all of that history, none of the layers.
