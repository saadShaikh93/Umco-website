Tax Advisory

Textile exporter recovers blocked sales-tax refunds

100% refunds released<90 day cycle
Challenge

A Karachi-based textile exporter had over a year of sales-tax refunds stuck in processing, straining working capital during peak season.

Solution

UMCPA reconstructed the refund claims with complete annexure documentation, resolved discrepancy notices, and represented the client through FBR's verification process.

Outcome

Refunds released in full; a documented claims process now keeps the refund cycle under 90 days.
