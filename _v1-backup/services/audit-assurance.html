Our Audit Services

What's included in our practice.

Statutory Audit

Mandatory audits conducted in full compliance with the Companies Act and SECP requirements, covering financial statement accuracy and internal control adequacy.

  • Annual financial statement audit
  • Regulatory compliance verification
  • Management letter with findings
  • Board presentation of results

Internal Audit

An objective, independent review of your internal controls, risk management processes, and governance frameworks to strengthen operational integrity.

  • Risk-based audit planning
  • Process and control evaluation
  • Fraud risk assessment
  • Recommendations and follow-up

Special Purpose Audits

Targeted audits for specific purposes — due diligence, grant compliance, donor reporting, or lender requirements — tailored to your unique circumstances.

  • Due diligence audits (M&A)
  • Grant and donor fund audits
  • Agreed-upon procedures
  • Compilation engagements
Our Process

How we conduct every engagement.

01

Planning & Risk Assessment

We study your business, industry, and prior year findings to build a risk-based audit plan tailored to what matters most.

02

Fieldwork & Testing

Our team performs substantive testing, analytical procedures, and control evaluations with minimal disruption to your operations.

03

Review & Reporting

Findings are reviewed by senior partners, and a clear audit report is issued — along with a management letter of actionable recommendations.

04

Debrief & Follow-up

We walk your leadership team through results in plain language and remain available to address any queries from regulators or lenders.
