What We Do

Tax strategy that works as hard as you do.

Pakistan's tax landscape is complex, frequently changing, and unforgiving of errors. UMCO's tax team combines deep FBR knowledge with proactive planning to ensure you're never caught off-guard — and always positioned to minimize your burden legally and efficiently.

From corporate tax structuring to sales tax representation, we handle the full spectrum of direct and indirect taxation for businesses of every size and sector.

Proactive Planning

We plan ahead — not just at year-end — to maximise available reliefs and exemptions throughout the tax year.

FBR Compliance

Full compliance with Federal Board of Revenue regulations — returns, audits, and dispute representation handled end-to-end.

Cross-Border Expertise

International tax structuring for businesses with cross-border operations, foreign investments, or import/export activity.
