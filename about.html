Our story

Why we started.

UMCO was founded in Karachi on a frustration familiar to anyone who has bought professional services in Pakistan: the partner who pitched the engagement disappears the day it is signed. We built the opposite — a firm where a chartered accountant with real authority stays on your file from first meeting to final deliverable.

Today the practice serves more than 500 businesses, from founder-led SMEs to subsidiaries of international groups, across audit, tax, advisory, outsourcing, and digital transformation.
